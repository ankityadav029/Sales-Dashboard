#  Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

This project presents an interactive **Sales Dashboard** created using **Tableau** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and payment trends. The dashboard provides a clear overview of key business metrics and helps identify patterns in product categories, customer segments, and sales channels.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights that help businesses understand **customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends**.

##  Key Dashboard Features

### Business KPIs

* **Total Sales:** 251M+
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 298K+
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 2,529
* **Total Invoices:** 99K+

### Sales Insights

* **Sales by Category** to identify top-performing product categories
* **Sales by Gender** to understand customer demographics
* **Sales by Payment Mode** to analyze customer payment preferences

### Trend Analysis

* **Monthly Sales Trend** to track seasonal patterns and sales fluctuations throughout the year

### Location Analysis

* **Sales by Shopping Mall** to compare performance across different retail locations

##  Key Insights

* Clothing and shoes contribute a major share of total sales.
* Female customers represent a larger portion of purchases.
* Cash and credit card are the most commonly used payment methods.
* Certain shopping malls generate significantly higher sales compared to others.
